Conference Notice


The "Sixth Trusted Software Systems Engineering (International) Spring School (SETSS 2024)" hosted by Southwest University will be held at Southwest University from April 15th to 21st, 2024. This spring the school will offer a series of lectures, academic reports and academic exchanges on cutting-edge research on software system engineering methods and tools, involving the most advanced methods and technological progress in the field of software engineering.


The spring school has invited renowned scholars such as Professor Moshe Y. Vardi, Professor Martin Georg Fränzle, Professor Kuldeep Meel, Professor Jean-Pierre Talpin and Professor Youcheng Sun. The event aims to facilitate in-depth discussions and extensive exchanges on interdisciplinary research combining disciplines and fields such as software engineering, formal methods, human-cyber-physcial systems, blockchain, and artificial intelligence.


Meeting time:

April 15-21, 2024


Meeting venue:

2 Tiansheng Road, Beibei District, Chongqing, Academic Lecture Hall 0114, No. 25, Southwest University.
