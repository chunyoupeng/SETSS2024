Recommended Tourist Attractions in Chongqing

The Yangtze River Cableway (长江索道)

Ride the aerial cable car across the Yangtze River for a panoramic view of the city's skyline and the majestic river below.

Ciqikou Ancient Town (磁器口古镇)

A historic town with traditional Chongqing architecture, famous for its teahouses, street snacks, and artisanal shops.

Dazu Rock Carvings (大足石刻)

A UNESCO World Heritage site with thousands of statues and carvings dating back to the 7th century, depicting and influenced by Buddhist, Confucian, and Taoist beliefs.

Jiefangbei CBD (解放碑商业区)

The bustling commercial heart of the city, home to the Jiefang Monument and surrounded by shopping malls, office buildings, and eateries.

Liziba Light Rail Station

Liziba Light Rail Station, a local internet-famous rail station where the light rail train passes directly through a residential building.

The Fairy Mountain National Forest Park

The Fairy Mountain National Forest Park is located within the territory of Wulong County in Chongqing and boasts the rare alpine meadow landscape of the south. Its forest sea, peculiar peaks, grasslands, and snowfields are referred to by tourists as the "Four Wonders" of Fairy Mountain.
